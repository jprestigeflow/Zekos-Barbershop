ABOUT ZEKO

ESTABLISHED 2015

Established in 2015, Owner Zeko has been proudly serving the Hamilton community for over 17 years, providing the ultimate barber experience. As Hamilton’s three-time top-choice award-winning barber shop, our priority is to offer best-in-class service while maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness.

Zeko’s convenient mountain location is walk-in friendly, offering a variety of services and products for men of all ages. We take pride in our commitment to excellence and look forward to welcoming you to our barbershop!
